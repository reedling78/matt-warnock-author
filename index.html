About the book

A door in the back of a magic shop

Jessie Barstow is a mostly ordinary girl with an extraordinary gift. She possesses the ability to travel to other worlds at will. It's a gift for which she's being hunted by sinister forces.

When she vanishes from her world and reappears in another, Jessie hopes that she's in the clear, but that won't stop the power-hungry sorceress, Demora, chasing Jessie down to get the girl's power for her own.

With the help of Martin, a down-on-his-luck magician who hasn't believed in real magic in a long time, Jessie races across strange and wondrous worlds toward the Source of All Magic itself — where she'll decide the fate of every world, including her own.

"Do you believe in magic?"

The author

Matt Warnock

Matt Warnock writes across a spectrum of speculative fiction. The Traveling Jessie Barstow is his debut novel. He has had micro-fiction appear in Vine Leaves Press 50 Give or Take, 50 Word Stories, and Ossuary 13.

Matt is a lifelong resident of Illinois, where he lives with his wife, children, and their dogs. When not writing, he enjoys hiking, gardening, and too many hobbies.
